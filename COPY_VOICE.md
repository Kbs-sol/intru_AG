# INTRU.IN — Brand Copy Voice Reference (v12)

This document defines the rules for all customer-facing microcopy on the intru.in platform.

## Core Tenets
1. **Terse & Confident**: Use fewer words. Never apologize for logistics.
2. **No Explanatory Bridges**: Do not write "Based on our return policy..." or "Since we ship from...". Just state the fact/power statement.
3. **Gen-Z Streetwear Native**: Use culture-appropriate terminology (Drop, Heat, Secure, Secured, Vaulted, Heat).
4. **Direct Response Psychology**: Focus on immediate action and psychological rewards (VIP, Priority, Skip the Queue).

## Key Terms & Mappings

| Element | Old/Generic | v12 Power Statement |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Newsletter Title** | Stay updated on our latest releases | WHERE SHOULD WE SEND YOUR DROP? |
| **Email Subtext** | Get notified of new drops | Secure your spot in the next limited release. No spam, just heat. |
| **Button CTA** | Subscribe | SECURE ACCESS |
| **Prepaid Order** | Save Rs. 99 | VIP TREATMENT: PRIORITY DISPATCH |
| **COD Order** | Rs. 99 Convenience Fee | LOGISTICS HEAVY (Rs. 99 FEE) |
| **Stock Status (Low)** | Few left | Low Stock: [X] left |
| **Stock Status (Critical)**| Almost sold out | CRITICAL: ONLY [X] LEFT |
| **Sold Out** | Out of Stock | VAULTED: SOLD OUT |
| **Shipping** | Orders ship in 2 days | 36H DISPATCH |

## Forbidden Phrases
- "Please" (Except in legal disclosure)
- "Unfortunately"
- "We are sorry to inform you" (Use: "DROP FAILED" or "NOT IN STOCK")
- "In order to" (Just start with the verb)
- "Our policy states" (Just state the policy)

## Design Specs
- **Casing**: Use UPPERCASE for primary CTAs and headings to convey authority.
- **Emphasis**: Use the ⚡ (bolt) emoji for speed/prepaid benefits and ⚠️ (warning) for COD friction.
